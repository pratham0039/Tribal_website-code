Guldasta: Ek Raag


“GULDASTA: EK RAAG” CULTURAL EVENING AT IIT JAMMU, CONVOCATION CEREMONY


IIT Jammu organized its 3rd convocation on 15-16 October. On 15 October, a cultural event titled Guldasta, Ek Raag or Bouquet of Music was presented in the campus. The institute’s Chairman, Dr Sharad Kumar Saraf, Director Manoj Gaur, along with the Registrar, members of BOG, and Senators, were present at the event. Director’s Tribal Chair announcement was done during the event, which has been established in association with Tribal Research Institute headed by Director Shahid Iqbal Choudhary and Deputy Director Dr. Abdul Khabur. Director felicitated Dr. Javaid Rahi, Chief Editor, Gojri Language, J&K Academy of Arts, Culture and Languages for his contribution to the tribal projects and courses at IIT Jammu.


Tribal Workshop


3-DAY TRIBAL WORKSHOP ORGANISED BY IIT JAMMU AND TRI, J&K


The Department of Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS), IIT Jammu, in collaboration with the Tribal Research Institute (TRI), J&K, organized a three-day workshop on “Tribal Praxis: Understanding Literary, Linguistic and Socio-cultural practices of the tribal communities of J&K” from 4th-6th July, 2022. The main objective of the workshop was to provide a platform for assimilation of ideas, theory and practice while developing a roadmap for fruitful collaboration between IIT Jammu and TRI, J&K. The Inaugural session to the three day workshop deliberated on the action points of the MoU between IIT Jammu and TRI, J&K, Tribal chair, and the targets and deliverables of the Tribal Project.


Research Collaboration


RESEARCH COLLABORATION BETWEEN IIT JAMMU AND TRIBAL RESEARCH INSTITUTE


With the focus to revitalize tribal cultures of J&K from a research point of view, IIT Jammu has signed an MoU with Tribal Research Institute. This MoU charters the establishment of Tribal Chair with targets as collection of tribal data, transcreation, digitization, dissemination, and on-site field study on the tribes of J&K, with an extensive focus on workshops, conferences, field visits, seminars, cultural programs, exhibitions, and other related activities..

Carnival - Ek Utsav


AN ORGANIC POTPOURRI OF J&K’S RICH CULTURE AT THE 2ND CONVOCATION OF IIT JAMMU.


The Carnival events consisted of following: Folk Dance by ML Dogra, Bhartiya Lok Sangeet Kala Sansthan, Sufi and Folk Songs by Sahil Lucky, Music Director, Sufi Soul Group, Medley by Dr. Ankit Kathuria, Faculty Member, IIT Jammu, Gojri Dance by J&K Academy of Arts & Culture: Gujjar Centre with Sadakat Ali and Javed Rahi, Poetry Recitation by Fatima Naz, Student, Computer Science Dept, IIT Jammu, Mushaira by Mr Hansal Azhar, Cultural Programme by Dr. Ankur Bansal, Faculty Member, IIT Jammu, Regional Performance by Vicky Gill, Music Director – Symphony Institute, “Sitaron Se Aage” Ghazal by Dr. Quleen Kaur Bijral, Faculty Member, IIT Jammu, Felicitation of Artists: Arts and Craft Felicitation, Cultural Exhibit: Binna Crafts and Basohli Paintings
